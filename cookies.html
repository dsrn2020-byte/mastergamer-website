Cookie Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how cookies and similar technologies may be used on the Master Gamer website and online experiences.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored by a browser. Similar technologies may include local storage, pixels, SDK identifiers, and analytics tags.

How Cookies May Be Used

Essential

Used for basic site operation, security, and session behaviour.

Analytics

Used to understand page usage, crashes, performance, and content improvements.

Media

Used by embedded trailers, video platforms, or social media features if enabled.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect embedded media, analytics, or online play features.

Contact

Cookie questions can be sent to info@mastergamer.com.au.
