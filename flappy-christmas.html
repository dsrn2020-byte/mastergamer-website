Flappy Christmas

A festive arcade adventure where Santa battles to save Christmas.

Overview

Save Christmas. Beat the Ice Giant.

The Ice Giant is trying to stop Christmas.

Guide Santa through an enchanted forest filled with ghosts, ghouls, scorpions, and dangerous enemies. Collect snowballs, unlock power-ups, and battle your way to an epic showdown with the Ice Giant.

A festive arcade adventure for players of all ages.

Features

  • Festive arcade adventure gameplay
  • Santa-themed flying action
  • Snowball power-ups
  • Enchanted forest enemies
  • Boss battle against the Ice Giant
  • Family-friendly Christmas fun
