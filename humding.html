Overview

Calm structure for busy minds

HumDing is a calm AI companion designed to help organise thoughts, ideas, reminders, and daily planning.

Capture information through text or voice and let HumDing transform scattered thoughts into structured daily clarity.

Designed to reduce mental clutter and help you focus on what matters most.

  • Daily summaries

  • Productivity support
  • Personal planning
