About

Independent Game & App Development Studio

Master Gamer is a Brisbane-based game and app development studio creating fun, accessible, and engaging experiences across mobile, browser, PC, and emerging platforms.

Since the release of our first title, Chicken Flapper, in 2018, we have continued developing a diverse range of games and applications spanning arcade action, puzzles, strategy, casual gaming, and productivity tools.

Today, Master Gamer is home to a growing collection of original games including Chicken Flapper, Sevens, TinyFall, HumDing and many more.

Our Mission

Create entertaining experiences that are easy to learn, rewarding to master, and enjoyable for players of all ages.

Brisbane, Australia

The studio operates from Brisbane and builds with a mobile-first, independent mindset shaped by Australia's growing game development community.
