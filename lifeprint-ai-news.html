MasterGamer Acquires LifePrint.ai

MasterGamer is excited to announce the acquisition of LifePrint.ai, an innovative AI-powered web platform focused on creating personalised digital experiences.

LifePrint.ai has been developed to help people engage with artificial intelligence in a more personal, meaningful, and intuitive way. Rather than offering a one-size-fits-all experience, the platform is designed to adapt to each individual, creating a unique digital companion that grows and evolves over time.

As part of the MasterGamer ecosystem, LifePrint.ai will soon be integrated directly into the MasterGamer portal, allowing users to seamlessly access both gaming and AI-powered experiences from a single platform.

This acquisition represents another important milestone in MasterGamer's vision of building an entertainment ecosystem that combines games, creativity, education, and artificial intelligence into one connected experience.
