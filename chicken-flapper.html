Overview

Have You Got What It Takes?

You are Chicken Flapper, battling your way through a haunted forest filled with terrifying creatures and powerful bosses.

Face rabid bats, mummified birds, giant spiders, demons, scorpions, and more as you fight through increasingly difficult stages. Collect power-ups, defeat enemy hordes, and survive epic boss battles on your journey through the forest.

A fast-paced arcade shooter packed with action, humour, and challenge.

Game Style

  • Haunted forest arcade action
  • Enemy hordes and boss battles
  • Power-ups and score chasing
  • Fast, funny, and challenging play
