Overview

Holiday mischief with quick-fire reflexes

Get ready for a festive tapping adventure in Slap Santa.

Journey through a playful Christmas world filled with cheeky elves, magical fairies, spirited reindeer, and plenty of holiday mischief. Test your reflexes in fast, funny slap battles where every tap counts.

Win battles, collect bonuses, boost your slapping power, and unlock new surprises as the world becomes brighter, wilder, and more festive.

With a final showdown against Santa himself, Slap Santa delivers light-hearted holiday fun for players of all ages.

Your finger's journey to Santa starts now.

  • Bonuses, power boosts, and unlockable surprises
