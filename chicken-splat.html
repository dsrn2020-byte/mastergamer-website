Overview

One hundred levels of comic arcade strategy

Guide your chickens through 100 increasingly challenging levels as you attempt to eliminate troublesome sheep using strategy, timing, and unique character abilities.

Unlock special chickens, discover power-ups, and prepare for the ultimate showdown against the Boss Sheep in Level 100.

A quirky arcade puzzle experience packed with humour and challenge.

Game Style

  • 100 increasingly challenging levels
  • Special chickens with unique abilities
  • Power-ups and timing-based shots
  • Boss Sheep finale in Level 100
