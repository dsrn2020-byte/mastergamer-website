Overview

Cannon-fired arcade chaos

Blast your chicken from a cannon and escape an army of mischievous monkeys in this fast-paced arcade adventure.

Collect power-ups, dodge obstacles, and fly as far as possible while chasing high scores and leaderboard glory.

Simple controls, colourful visuals, and endless gameplay make Chicken Blast easy to pick up and hard to put down.

Game Style

  • Cannon-launch arcade action
  • Power-ups and obstacle dodging
  • High-score chasing
  • Colourful, fast replayable sessions
