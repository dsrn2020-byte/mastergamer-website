Overview

Chicken-powered kung fu combat

Take Wing Chun Chicken straight into your browser and fight through waves of arcade enemies with quick reactions, sharp timing, and a glorious amount of KIAI power.

Fly-kick through hazards, punch through enemy waves, collect power-ups, and push toward boss battles across 100 levels. It is built for quick testing on desktop and touch play on mobile.

Features

  • Playable online browser build
  • Retro arcade kung fu combat
  • Fly-kicks, punches, and KIAI power
  • Power-ups and wave clearing
  • Boss battles across 100 levels
  • Keyboard, mouse, and touch support
