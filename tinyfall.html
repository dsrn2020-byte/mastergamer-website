TinyFall

Pop fast. Stack smart. Don't crack.

A colourful block-clearing puzzle game built for quick arcade fun, simple rules, and satisfying score chasing.

Overview

Colourful puzzle action

TinyFall is a colourful block-clearing puzzle game built for quick arcade fun.

Tap groups of matching blocks before the stack reaches the top. Every move matters--single blocks won't clear, so strategy and timing are key.

Choose from Easy, Medium, Hard, or Pro modes and challenge yourself to achieve higher scores and faster clears.
