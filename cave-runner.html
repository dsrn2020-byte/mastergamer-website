Overview

Run deeper into the darkness

Deep beneath the earth lies the legendary lost gold of Alcatraz, hidden within a maze of ancient caves, deadly traps, and forgotten treasures.

As the Cave Runner, you'll dodge hazards, leap across dangerous terrain, avoid collapsing caverns, and battle your way through increasingly challenging underground worlds in search of untold riches.

Every level brings new obstacles, greater dangers, and bigger rewards as you push deeper into the darkness.

Features

  • Classic side-scrolling adventure gameplay
  • Dangerous traps and hazards
  • Hidden treasure and secrets
  • Multiple cave environments
  • Increasing difficulty and challenge
  • Fast-paced arcade action
